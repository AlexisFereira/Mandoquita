# Category imagery

Approved taxonomy category images for `CategoryCard`.

## Delivery contract

- Canvas: 1600 × 1000 px (16:10, 2× the rendered 800 × 500 media size).
- Format: PNG, sRGB.
- Crop safety: the meaningful product group stays inside the central 75% so the compact 4:3 variant remains usable with `object-cover`.
- Art direction: warm editorial product still life, soft natural light, restrained coral/cream/graphite palette, no text, logos, watermarks, people, or promotional claims.
