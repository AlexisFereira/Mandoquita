## Business Boundary

This content is informational only.

It does not:

- select a payment method;
- validate a method;
- calculate an amount, conversion, fee, exchange rate, or balance;
- collect payment or financial information;
- initiate checkout, payment, reservation, order, receipt, or account behavior;
- guarantee that one method applies to every Product or situation without direct confirmation.

## Brand and Icon Boundary

- The public name `Binance` is approved as text.
- No Binance trademark asset or third-party payment logo is approved by this decision.
- The Design System may use domain-neutral supporting Icons only when they do not imply another payment brand or transactional integration.
- Every method remains understandable without an Icon.
