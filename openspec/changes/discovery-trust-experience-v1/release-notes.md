# Discovery and Trust Experience V1 — Release Notes

Status: Released

Date: 2026-07-13

## Delivered

- Public Product Search at `/buscar`, backed by the canonical Product collection.
- Governed semantic Icons through the shared Design System component.
- Static Homepage `Medios de pago` information for Binance, Pago móvil and
  Dólares en efectivo, with optional WhatsApp continuation.
- Accessible, visible-by-default Scroll-entry Motion on the three approved
  supporting sections.

## Preserved Boundaries

The release adds no cart, checkout, online payment, order, authentication or
payment-provider integration. Commercial Availability protection, Product and
Variant rules, taxonomy eligibility, responsive behavior, accessibility and the
deterministic light-only presentation remain authoritative.

## Validation

26 test files/148 tests, TypeScript and the optimized production build pass.
Independent Search, taxonomy, Product-content, lifecycle, accessibility,
responsive, motion and performance evidence is recorded in the change artifact.
